       * Organs: Thymus (~75% LOGO Acc), Heart (~70% LOGO Acc), Skin (~64% LOGO Acc).

       * Behavior: Consistently high accuracy regardless of the experimental batch or random seed.
       * Conclusion: These represent universal cellular stress responses to microgravity. The foundation model has
         learned that "Spaceflight" fundamentally means a shift in mitochondrial energy production and protein
         translation machinery.
